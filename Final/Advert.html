Property summary

This stunning and modernised chateau is an ideal Bed and Breakfast and gite complex including semi professional kitchen, breakfast room, pool, sauna, and offering an immediate source of revenue. The property is very private down a long drive, and is perched on a sloping hillside in the middle of its land. Most apartments have external access, and each have their own kitchenettes, private terraces and one large space for parking. Managers apartment with office is in place.
